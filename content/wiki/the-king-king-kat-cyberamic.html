History

The King was created by Kathy Wolf and Jul Kamen sometime in 1981. He first appeared in {{< wiki-link "Pinole, CA (1470 Fitzgerald Dr)" >}}(1), and later also appeared in {{< wiki-link "Loves Park, IL (6505 N 2nd St)" >}} (2) , Memphis, Tennessee (3), Reno, NV and in the Hong Kong location (4), He was installed inside the lounge and performed Elvis Presley songs. Before the bankruptcy of Pizza Time Theatre, the company tried to save with the King Kat, which was King Animatronics, whose theme changed from Elvis Presley to Michael Jackson. The King Kat also got brand-new clothes to fit his new theme.

Design

He is a lion with brown fur, and as the King, he wears a white jumpsuit with golden accents and has black hair styled in a pompadour style. As the King Kat, he wears a red jacket with golden accents and has black and curly hair that goes to his shoulder.
