History

Origins

He was a parody of Wolfman Jack and was voiced by Aaron Fetcher, he was the emcee of the show and would provide the vocals. He was a silver wolf with a mustache, and he wore a variety of tuxedos depending on the location and was holding a microphone.

Wolfpack 5

The wolfman talked like a classic radio announcer and would announce each music section with a short sentence; he was also the leader of the band. The Wolfman's friends thought he was a fool for living in the past; he also has an unnamed father and an agent (2).
