History

Dolli Dimple was the first side act in the piano bar, which is today known as the Cabaret Room. This version was the second generation of {{< wiki-link " Kooser Dolli Dimples" >}}, and was first used in {{< wiki-link " Concord, CA (1975 Diamond Blvd)" >}} and was subsequently used for restaurants that opened after it. On occasion she was also replaced by {{< wiki-link "Artie Antlers (Cyberamic)" (1)>}} and {{< wiki-link "B.B. Bubbles (Cyberamic)" >}}(2)

Design

She is a grey hippopotamus with bright orange hair that is styled into an afro and wearing a lilac and silver floral dress with a shawl and two rings on each finger.
